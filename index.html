Elon Reeves Musk

The African-American entrepreneur pushing the boundaries of technology and humanity

Here is a timeline of Elon Musk's life and accomplishments:

  • 2002 — Founded SpaceX with the goal of reducing space transportation costs.
  • 2008 — Tesla releases the all-electric Roadster.
  • 2012 — SpaceX's Dragon becomes the first commercial spacecraft to deliver cargo to the ISS.
  • 2015 — Announces the Powerwall battery product for homes and businesses.
  • 2016 — Co-founds Neuralink to integrate brains with AI.
  • 2017 — Launches The Boring Company to develop underground transit systems.
  • 2020 — Becomes the world's richest person (briefly) and launches multiple Starlink satellites.
  • 2022 — Acquires Twitter and begins transforming the platform.

“Engineering is the closest thing to magic that exists in the world.”

— Elon Musk
